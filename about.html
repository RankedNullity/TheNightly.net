Jaron Wang

Creator

Hello, I'm Jaron Wang, the creator of this website. I was born in Beijing, China, and moved to California when I was about eight years old. There, my family and I moved from place to place every year until I landed in Davis, California. I stayed there for about two years, until my family decided to relocate to Washington. I stayed in Bellevue until the end of that year, then moved to Redmond, where I currently reside. As you can tell, I've been around. However, I quite like the climate in Washington, so I don't think I'd be moving much more.

Education

I went to high school at Redmond High School (Washington) and with the help of some of the most wonderful teachers I've ever met, I found my passion for all things science and technology. And if that sounds broad, that's because it is. I took AP class after AP class, hoping to narrow down my passions; instead, I only added to the list. By the time I graduated, I was left with more questions than answers.

The fall after graduation, I started attending the University of Washington with my ego a little too bloated from high school. That quarter, I took 22 credits while working full time as a sous chef. Needless to say, my hubris took its toll. As crazy as I am, it was still a little much, even for me. Following that, I decided to take it easy and take some intro-level courses, so I could find my true interests. Then, I heard this quote somewhere.

"If you ever have to make a decision, flip a coin. Bet on the outcome, and if you regret the decision you made, then you should chose the other option."

Which is essentially what I did. The whole time I was taking other classes, I just felt myself craving for a math class. So that was eventually what I decided on. Now, I'm currently a third year double majoring in Applied and Compuational Mathematical Sciences (ACMS) and Theoretical Mathematics with a minor in Physics.
